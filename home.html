AN R&B PARTY INSPIRED BY SOME OF THE BEST TUNES
FROM THE 90'S AND EARLY 2000's

The 4 Lovers Only Tour is a memorable experience from beginning to end. Each city will feature a sponsored one hour open bar, 90’s R&B all night and special guest performances from R&B’s legends and some fresh faces.

4 Lovers Only has also attracted major star power, with 90s R&B legend Adina Howard, TLC songstress T-Boz and star in BET’s The New Edition story, Keith Powers.

4 Lovers Only will be in Los Angeles, New York, Dallas, Miami for the first leg of the tour. The tour is set to feature a special guest performance by R&B newcomer Tone Stith, iconic DJ sets from of our tour DJ, DJ 9AM, Soulection’s Sasha Marie, Austin Millz, viral sensation Ash B and many many more.
