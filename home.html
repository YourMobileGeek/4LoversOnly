AN R&B PARTY INSPIRED BY SOME OF THE BEST TUNES
FROM THE 90'S AND EARLY 2005

The 4 Lovers Only Tour is an experience created to deliver an entire decade of impactful music, an open bar & free merchandise to thousands of people in select cities across the country.

4 Lovers Only also attracted major star power, with 90's R&B legend Adina Howard, TLC songstress T-Boz and star in BET's The New Edition Story, Keith Powers all in attendance in various cities.

In 2018, 4 Lovers Only will be bigger & better as hit L.A, NYC, Dallas & Miami with the city's best DJ's, talent and 90's R&B legends!
